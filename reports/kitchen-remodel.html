What Gets Blocked — And Why

Not every lead deserves your team's time. These rules fire before the scoring model runs, protecting you from the worst leads in any dataset.

⛔ Instant Rejects — Never Delivered

These leads are removed entirely. You never see them, and you never pay for them.

  • Invalid phone number — number does not exist or cannot receive calls
  • Bot detected — automated submission identified through behavioral analysis
  • Pre-populated form — lead data was injected, not entered by a human
